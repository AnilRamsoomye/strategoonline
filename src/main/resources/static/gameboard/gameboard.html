Setup:
To add pieces to the board, first click on a cell you would like to add a piece to then click on the keys 0 ~ 9, "F", or "B".
To remove a piece select the cell and press Delete
During the setup phase you may only place pieces on your side of the board
Once you have played all of your pieces press the Enter key to start the game.
You can see how many pieces of each type you have left to place at the bottom of the screen.
Pieces:
F = Flag, Immovable, Loses against every piece, if destoryed the opponent wins.
1 = Spy, Loses against every piece excent the Marshal(if the Spy attacks the Marshal).
2 = Scount, Can teleport any distance, in a straight l
3 = Miner, Wins against Bomb.
4 = Sergeant.
5 = Lieutenant.
6 = Captain.
7 = Major.
8 = Colonel.
9 = General.
10 = Marshal wins against every piece. Loses against the Spy if attacked by a spy
B = Bomb, Immovable, Wins against every piece excent the Miner.
How To Play:
You can only move one piece per turn.
Pieces may move one square per turn(unless they are a scout)
Pieces can move up down left or right. Not diagonally.
Pieces have their identites hidden unless they get into battle
When a piece moves into another piece it enters combat. If a piece is a rank higher than its opponents it wins, if they are the same rank both parties lose. If it is lower it loses.
